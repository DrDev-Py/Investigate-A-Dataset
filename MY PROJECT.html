Introduction 🔰¶

This dataset collects information from 100,000 medical appointments in Brazil and is focused on the question of whether or not patients show up for their appointment.

It contains a number of characteristics about each person that has an appointment. Characteristics include:

  • Patient ID Identification number of the patient
  • Appointment ID The identification of each apppointment.
  • Gender This can be either male or female
  • Scheduled Day This is the day the appointment was scheduled
  • Appointment Day This is the actual day of the appointment
  • Age This is the age of the patient
  • Neighbourhood shows the area where a patient lives.
  • Scholarship This tells us whether they are on Scholarship or not.
  • Hypertension This indicates whether they are hypertensive or not, 1 for yes, 0 for no.
  • Diabetes This indicates whether they are diabetic or not, 1 for yes, 0 for no.
  • Alcoholism This indicates whether they are alcoholic or not, 1 for yes, 0 for no.
  • Handcap This indicates whether they are handicapped or not.
  • SMS_received This indicates whether they are hypertensive or not, 1 for yes, 0 for no.
  • No_show This indicates whether they showed up for the appointment or not. No if they showed up, Yes if they didn't

Questions ❓¶

    I would determine the difference in the appointment and schedule days of each appointment and condouct a summary statistics to determine the time difference

Conclusions ✔✔¶

1. Males and Females almost equally miss their appointments. Males 28.7% of the time while males 28.4%

 2. Those who missed their appointments were slightly younger than those who showed up, with the mean ages being 34.84 and 39.97 years respectively

3. Those who were on scholarships were not more likely to miss appointments than those who weren't.

 4. People who received SMS reminders were more likely to show up on their appointment day

5. Those that set appointment dates farther in the future were more likely to miss their appointments than those who set their own closer.
